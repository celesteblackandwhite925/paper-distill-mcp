## 🚀 What is paper-distill-mcp?

paper-distill-mcp is a simple program to help you find, organize, and push academic papers across different platforms. It runs on your Windows computer and works without needing technical skills. Use it to quickly search for papers, save your favorites, and send them to your devices or apps.

---

## 🖥️ System Requirements

Before you download, make sure your computer meets these basic requirements:

- Windows 10 or newer  
- At least 4 GB of RAM  
- 500 MB of free disk space  
- Internet connection (to search and download papers)  
- A web browser (like Edge, Chrome, or Firefox)

## 🎯 How to Use paper-distill-mcp

### Search for Papers

- Type a topic or keyword in the search box.  
- Click **Search**.  
- A list of papers matching your search will appear.

### Save Papers

- Click the **Save** button next to any paper you want to keep.  
- Saved papers show up in your **Library** for easy access later.

### Push Papers

- Select papers you want to send to another platform.  
- Choose where to send: email, cloud storage, or reading app.  
- Click **Send** and wait for confirmation.

- Run the installer as Administrator (right-click the file and select **Run as administrator**).

- Restart your computer and try again.

If the app opens but does not work as expected:

- Make sure you have entered your push settings correctly.  
- Restart the app and try again.
